Cocktails

Do you really know what you’ve been drinking? After studying a survey about cocktails, I have learned that most people are not aware of what their drinks contain, especially college students. Hence I create a cocktail info-graphic design that could be used as a leave-behind at bars/restaurants for casual reading. It includes a short study on the popular cocktails, alcohol percentage, as well as a history of cocktails origin.

Size: 5 x 20 inch, Accordion Fold

Cocktail Research:


  • Alcohol Percentage
  • Amount of Calories
  • Year of Creation
  • Ingredients
  • Cocktail Glasses

Data Visualization, Illustration

Software: Illustrator, R Studio, InDesign, Acrobat

Color palette

This graph presents the time of origin, alcohol percentage, calories level, and type of alcohol consisted in each cocktail. According to the ranking, the most popular pick is Caipirinha, followed closely by Mai Tai. Caipirinha, roughly translates to "country bumpkin," is Brazil’s national cocktail, made with cachaca-sugar cane hard liquor, sugar and lime.

As tasty as they are, cocktails contain high calories and high alcohol level. Sex on the Beach, Caipirinha, and Mai Tai are high in calories, above 300 calories each. Mahattan, Apple Martini, and Old Fashioned cocktails all share an alcohol percentage higher than 33% each.
