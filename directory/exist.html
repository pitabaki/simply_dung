Exist

A Memorial Project for Bei Dao

Bei Dao was an underground poet fighting for Capitalism in a Communist Nation. Born and raised under Communism, he saw the heights and lows of life, and he wanted change. Quiet and determined, he presented a political solution that turned him into a national traitor, exiled for life.

Deliverable: Poster (30 x 40 inch), Book (5.25 x 7.5 inch), Trailer


Typography, Illustration

Software: Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Acrobat

Color palette

Poster

Poster promotes and revokes curiosity about the event for Bei Dao. It should stand out from far away, either on its own or among other posters. The event focuses on Bei Dao’s political idea and decision that led him to be both the hero and the trailor of his people. Following his methodology, there is little to be shown. The poster is clean, minimal, but disrupted, just like the movement he created. Bei Dao was an underground poet who rejected Communism because of the poverty and inequality he had seen. He fought for Capitalism with the pen as his sword and literature as his weapon.

Book

The Book tells the life story of Bei Dao, his poems, his life after the movement. Unlike the poster, the book is for heavy-reading, with historical details and emotional transition.

Chapter Openers

Since the book follows Bei Dao from when he was born and raised under Communism, breaking out of his shell, etc., toward the exile that he is suffering, the chapter openers is an animation of his transition, using words and number to distort the shape and create distance.
