Website Projects

Various Website assets created for Tradeshift to host and promotes their events, webinars, campaigns, and new product features. Targets worldwide audience, mostly in Northern America (San Francisco) and Europe (London, Paris). The design follows and strengthens the brand’s style guide: sleek, modern, energetic, straight to the point and comprehensive.

Landing Pages for Events and Webinars

Landing pages are single, stand-alone pages that have been designed to focus on a single objective. Most landing pages at Tradeshift promote events, webinars, and store resources. The design shapes and limit the information to only the target audience, who are directed to the page, toward our intend conversion goal.

Event & Webinar Center (with 2 subpages)

Hosting and promoting every webinars and events from Tradeshift, the Center page needs to follows the brand’s style without overpowering the main informations it’s hosting. Therefore the design of the page itself is simple, yet highly practical as in highlighting the events and offering a filter according to the audience’s interest. Past webinars are presented as well as a link to our Resource Center, where tutorials and demo are recorded.
