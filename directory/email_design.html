Email Templates Projects

Email Templates serve as announcement, update, a method to keep touch with clients and build a connection. Despite the various purposes, all emails have to portray the brand, following Tradeshift’s identity guidelines. Every elements on the layout has to trigger an action by clients.


Marketing, Illustration, Typography

Software: Photoshop, Illustrator

Company: Tradeshift

Color palette

Event Invitation

Event invitation has to be informative: introduction to the event, the speakers, the agenda, sponsors, location and time. Messages are presented in hierachy. Heavy content resulted in a long email, therefore CTAs placements are important. Two CTAs are placed: one at the beginning, when most of the key info are presented, and one at the end of the email, when clients understand more about the event, leading to moment of decision.

Webinar Announcement & Reminder

Webinars at Tradeshift are mostly Finance Process tutorials and product demos. There are appropriately 3 webinars every month, 2 sections (US and EU timezone) running 30 minutes each. Since there is a repetition, the emails should be simple and short. Webinar Announcement is straight-forward, with 2 CTAs: add to calendar, or register. Reminders will be sent a day before the webinar, confirming the final attendance. The design is consistent to show that they both belong to a series.
