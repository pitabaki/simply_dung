GoDigital Campaign

A movement to introduce digitalization in AP process. Tradeshift offers a platform that assist financial process and provides a network for P2P. The campaign points out benefits of digitalization and presents a kit for businesses to go digital: business case calculator, infographic, etc.

Anti-Ariba Campaign

A social media attack when Ariba, one of Tradeshift’s biggest competitor, has its annual conference in San Francisco. The goal is to offer a different approach to AP process, go paperless, and free network between suppliers and buyers. The campaign appears on various social networks: LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, Google Plus.
