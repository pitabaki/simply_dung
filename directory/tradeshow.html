Tradeshow Projects

Tradeshift participates in several tradeshows throughout the year. Some target specific audience; some promote the brand and our idea. The purpose is to create a brand-awareness in a crowded area.

Booths

Tradeshows are good opportunity to show one’s product, find leads and engage potential clients to what the company has to offer. The challenges I face is to make the brand stand out in an environment where every other competitor in the same industry is present. The design is bold and eye-catchy, but on-brand and not too rebelious.

Give-aways

Along with all the digital campaigns, Tradeshift also presents tangible products at trade shows for clients to take with them. These give-aways can be fun daily objects, branded with Tradeshift with a clever message. One of the company’s favorite give-aways is a band-aid box. It is a crucial first-aid product that is good to keep side-by-side. As Tradeshift is promoting online softwares to replace manual paper-invoice process, our team completes the band-aid design with a punch line embracing the company’s slogan: Shift Happens, paper-cuts don’t have to.
