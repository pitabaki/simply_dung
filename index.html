Dung Nguyen

a designer • a tech-enthusiast • a curious mind

Dung Nguyen is a graphic designer, a traveler, and an explorer. She graduated with honors at San Francisco State University majoring in Visual Communication Design. As a creative, she believes that design functions as a tool of communication. It is a way to solve the problem, to deliver complicated data, message and information. Her design aesthetic is a harmony of boldness and minimalism.

Dung loves morning coffee and waffles!
